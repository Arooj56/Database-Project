Business Semantics:
Entities	
Patient			A person receiving treatment or using hospital services.
Login			Authentication credentials used by a patient to securely access the system.
Appointment		A scheduled meeting between a patient and a doctor.
Previous Records	Historical medical data like Prescriptions, Checkups and Payments, viewable by the patient.
Order Medicines		A request by the patient to purchase prescribed medicine.
Payment			A financial transaction made by the patient for appointments or medicines.
Payment Confirmation	Acknowledgment that a payment was successfully received and recorded in the system.
